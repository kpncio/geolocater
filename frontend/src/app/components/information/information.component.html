Cookies & Sessions

Cloudflare Services

Locale does not read, write, or interact with any cookies, local sessions, or local storage; however, KPNC Technology uses Cloudflare services to provide users with the best experience and to protect our own infrastructure. Cloudflare sits in between your client and our origin servers and may read, write, or interact with their own cookies, local sessions, or local storage for a variety of reasons.

For more information see Cloudflare's Privacy Policy...

Technologies

Cloudflare Workers, Maxmind GeoIP, IP2Location (Lite), Angular

The backend of Locale uses both Cloudflare Workers with Maxmind's GeoIP information for fast and scalable data retrieval and a local relational database with IP2Location's various databases for a more in-depth lookup. The frontend of Locale uses Google's Angular framework for a powerful and performant experience.

See more information at Angular, Maxmind, IP2Location, and Workers..

Map Visualization

Leaflet, Mapbox, OpenStreetMap

KPNC Locale uses a map suit including Leaflet for client-side map rendering, Mapbox for terrain tiles, and OpenStreetMap for street information.

See more information at Leaflet, Mapbox, and OpenStreetMap..
